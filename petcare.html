General Dog Care Instructions

A dog can be a wonderful addition to any home, but it's important to prioritize their health and happiness. Feed puppies and dogs according to their age, and always provide fresh water. Exercise, grooming, and proper handling are essential for their well-being. Ensure they have a warm, quiet place to rest, and follow your communityâ€™s licensing regulations, including ID tags and microchips. Regularly check for fleas and ticks, and never give medications not prescribed by a vet. Spay or neuter your dog by six months, and keep vaccinations up to date. Equip your home with necessary supplies like quality dog food, a leash, and a cozy bed. Always clean up after your dog in public spaces. Consider adopting a dog from a shelter to give a pet in need a loving home.
